Phineus Magician for Hire,
Phineus meets Möltaire


By John M. Dean

Based on original characters created by Barry Linck

Background: Phineus is a magician; for hire. He and his wife Sarah hunt vampires and other paranormal nuisances. Comic book author, Barry Linck created and publishes these characters. The following script was for a collaborative effort between Barry and myself.

And so, Phineus meets Moltaire.
