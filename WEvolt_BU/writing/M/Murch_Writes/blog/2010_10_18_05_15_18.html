Today we, in some ways, reset the clock for Jyk.  This arc is a little over a cycle(year) from the previous, although that interim encapsulates the period of time in which he matures far beyond the chronology.  A "defining year," perhaps.  This is at the beginning of that process.  

When you met Jyk, he was a promising young soldier.  Here he is still the lowest of servants in Tarrant Castle.  I have not referenced it much (most of that material is in the novel manuscript), but Jyk is orphan to a pair of castle servants.  As such he was raised by castle servants and given the stature of their own scullion.

Recently Jyk had been too happy to get into trouble - first love and all that - but now we find him fresh off of the inevitable heartbreak.  After a bad moment cost him a few lashes, he decided to seek a more organized and fruitful place to vent his anger and frustration - prize fighting.
