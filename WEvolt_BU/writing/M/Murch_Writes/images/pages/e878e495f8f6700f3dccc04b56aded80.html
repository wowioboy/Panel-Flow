Notes on the Calendar

 

The calendar in this world is not like our modern calendar, in fact is much more related to the structure of the Mayan calendar.

Span – Nine Days

Month – Three Span

Cycle – Fifteen Months

Generation – Twenty-seven Cycles

Grand Cycle – Fifteen Generations

Eon – Nine Grand Cycles

Few but longer lived races, clergy, and monks pay attention to the long date, but its structure is as follows:

(1-9) - (0-14) - (0-26) - (0-14) - (0-26)

Grand Cycle of Eon ; Generation of Grand Cycle ; Cycle of Generation ; Month of Cycle ; Day of Month
