Pop Galaxy Covers the Weekly Rumble!

 

You guys know about the Weekly Rumble over on Wevolt.com don't you?  Wait. What?  How in the hell do you not know about the Weekly Rumble?  And you call yourself a nerd too? Ok.  Maybe I pulled out my nerd cred whooping' stick a little too prematurely.  Webcomics creators…. are you ready to Rumble? 

Hells-to -the- YEAH!

Ok, noobs.  If you don't know what Wevolt's Weekly Rumble is? Well, with Zuda folding up shop, many webcomic creators and enthusiasts hung their heads low. Had all the opportunities for the little guys and indie lovers dried up, died, and been blown away in the wind? Absolutely not!

Enter stage left, WEvolt.com’s Weekly Rumble. A straight forward contest wecomic creators. Upload your comic to WEvolt.com, get the most Facebook likes, win $100 for the week. On top of that, the CREATORS keep all rights to their work.

The weekly winners for each bracket (there are 3 brackets at 3 weeks long) go head-to-head for the big prize, $500. Done and done.  Check out this recap of weeks 1-3, so you can jump right in and keep up with the action.

Week 1 kicked off with 18 competitors, a few were already WEvolt heavy lifters like James Riot’s "The Path" or Barry Linck’s "Phinmagic," but quite a few new guys came in and hit the ground running.  Newcomer Scotty Richard’s "Blaed Race" took an early lead quickly followed by IsenMike’s "The First Law of Mad Science."

In the end week 1 was SWEPT by James Mascia’s "High School Heroes," followed by "The Path" and "The First Law of Mad Science."

James Mascia’s High School Heroes

Week 2 saw new competitor, "Acting Out Comics," enter the ring. Week 2 ran through much like Week 1 had, with a few of the new comics taking an early lead, holding their own with WeVolt veterans throughout the week. Despite an intense back and forth race throughout the week, Week 2 was won by "High School Heroes," followed again by "The Path" and WEvolt veteran D.R. Croston’s "Method Comix."

James Riot's The Path:


 
Week 3 is underway and everyone is watching closely.

Voting closes Friday at midnight PST and we all anxiously await to see if there is an upset in the dynasty set up by High School Heroes. Or after 2 weeks of  near wins if The Path rises up to take Week 3 for it’s own.

It’s all up to YOU, head over to WEvolt.com, read some great comics and “like” your favs. Tune back Monday to see the winners announced.
