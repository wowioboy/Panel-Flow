Adding A Buy This On WOWIO Button

Today’s blog is a tutorial introducing another great new feature. If you currently have an item on sale at WOWIO you can add a “buy this now” widget in a few simple steps.

 

1.     While logged in, click into “CMS Dashboard” from the upper most menu.  Choose the “edit” icon on the project you want to add a WOWIO button too.


2.     Click “Manage Content” from the CMS menu.


3.     Choose to “edit template settings” of the page you’d like to add the button


4.     Above the “AVAILABLE MODULES” menu choose “Create new module”


5.     A pop up will appear, from the drop down menu choose “WOWIO BOOKS option


6.     You’ll return to the module editing screen and see a new WOWIO BOOKS  module in the available modules menu.  You can click the “EDIT” to change how you see the alignment of the WOWIO button within the display module.

7.     Lastly from the AVAILABLE MODULES menu drop the WOWIO BOOKS module over to where you’d like it to be place within your template. REMEMBER you MUST click  the yellow “Save Order” button to  finalize your changes.


8.      Click the save icon at the top of the page and there you have it, a nice Buy This Now WOWIO button for you comic
