NEW FEATURE: Page Mover and More!

Series-When your comic has several different …well series. If the X-men was on WEvolt, each spin off would be a different series. So Uncanny X-men, X-Men Adventures, Ultimate X-Men, X-Men: Evolved, X-Men: Noir....you get the point. Each one of these would be an individual SERIES.

 

Episode-Think of this as a story arc.  A large section of your story that needs to be sectioned off, but can be divided up even smaller, like into…

 

Chapters-this is pretty self-explanatory. It can be entire 22 page issues or larger or smaller chapters. However you want to break it up. Divide and conquer people!

So let me guess, you love it and you wanna reorganize your comic? (Hell *I* did!) Well it’s even simpler than you think! Go to your CMS, click on your project, click EDIT pages…BAM! You’re there. On the left you’ll notice a few new menu options. You can use the “New Episode” and “New Series” to do the obvious, BUT if you click “Move Pages” you’ll get a nifty new interface that lists out all of your comic pages and lets you move them into any of these new sections you’ve created.

 

And that is how easy it is to organize your comic on WEvolt!
