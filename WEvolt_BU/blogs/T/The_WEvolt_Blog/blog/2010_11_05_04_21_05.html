Week 2 Rumble; Post Your Milestones and Con Choices

It’s Friday and Week Two Rumblers only have today today to collect “likes” so if you’re a fan of one (or many) of these comics make sure to be a pal and keep an eye on ‘em over the weekend and “like” their most recent pages.  Creators! Remember all the tools you have at your disposal. Tweet to your fans! Send out that newsletter! Do something with that Facebook group! People won’t know to “like” your most recent update unless you tell them to, so get to it!

When your comic reaches some sort of milestone feel free to post it here. We’ll be keeping a close eye on this forum and make sure to tweet/post about it so you’ll get the recognition you deserve! You may even get included in our newest feature The Weekly Wrap Up!
