Welcome to Friday! We’re covering the last half of the top 10 here at WEvolt and hope to fill your weekend with comic reading goodness.  So let’s get to it and dive into what will be owning MY leisure time this weekend!

 

The Unwanted: It’s like taking the BEST parts of a horror movie, pairing it with fabulous art, packaging it up, and giving it to you to read online for free. Trapped inside a juvenile detention center, a group of street-smart teens battle for their souls against a demon that feeds on society's "unwanted" kids!

 

American Wasteland: A wonderful title from publisher Arcana about a gnarly trucker up against even gnarlier vampire like creatures. His name is even Cletus! This story is great when you need to get a fix of blood and guts and the good guys taking down the bad guys with an axe and a hack saw!

 

Blaed Race: This beautifully drawn comic provides you with 100% of your daily recommended sci fi and space adventure. Planet Blaed has held a contest of battle to determine rulership for thousands of years. This law is called the Race, but this law will be challenged by one unlikely resident and a new movement will emerge.

 

Creepy Carly: No no, that’s not a nifty Photoshop effect, this single panel chibi comic about an unusual little girl is really drawn on yellow lined paper with a blue Pilot G-2-07 pen. This comic is a cute and funny strip about a murderous, sadistic little girl that kills people and tortures animals. Yes, we said cute and funny. See for yourself.

 

WEvolt Trinity:The Path’s creator James Riot teamed up with Phinmagic’s creator Barry Linck to create a crossover comic of EPIC proportions. Phinmagic’s Sarah and  Phinus team up with The Path’s Chance to fight the forces of evil and all that is Cthulhu and Lovecraftian, or for short, more awesome that you can shake a stick at.

So that’s the WEvolt top 10! Some are in the Rumble some are not, regardless they’ve been proven by the masses to be great reads. So check them out and vote for your favorites!
