Today’s post is about the best of the best on WEvolt. We want to focus on the stories that the fans are loving on WEvolt and see if a quick review will get you to give these a look also. So let’s do a break down of the top 5. Tomorrow we’ll cover the other half of the Top Ten comics at WEvolt!

 

#1 The Path: What was once amazing, is now even better. James Riot has relaunched and reloaded the path and it's jaw-dropping. Head on over, take a look. Cthulhu, Arthurian legend, action, adventure, great art. Really, what more do you need?

 

#2 High School Heroes: James Mascia's latest story arc, "Strong" is arguably the best art the series has seen. Throw in some character sketches from Jason and you've got a ton of eye candy to go with his great story.

 

#3 Phinmagic: Proving that your first name doesn't have to be "James" to be popular on WEvolt is Barry. But ten years of punching this story out with great art is a mountain of fantastic material to dig into.

 

#4 Mohagen: Arguably the best strip comic on WEvolt. Hilarious, dark and beautifully drawn. How interesting is life in a fish bowl? Wait and see. It’s only fault is that I can’t read it everyday, but a strip this well executed can’t be rushed. It takes time so savor, enjoy, and damn it vote!

 

#5 Olive Peril: Is one of our Arcana titles. It’s a pro comic brought to you for free by WEvolt and it’s damn good. I read it daily. Girl gets stuck in a crazy situation and…um a fairy thing helps her out. Kinda sorta, hard to explain, have to read it for yourself. Beautifully drawn and such a great read.
