So yesterday we showed you how to get your comic project live and you’re ready
to Rumble. Wait…hold up, you’ve still got some questions on the logistics (you
did read the FAQ, right?) Here are a few more in depth questions answered
about the Weekly Rumble.
How is this different than Zuda?
We don't keep ANY of your rights! We only ask that you give WEvolt a try and
enjoy yourself! Hopefully you'll find some new fans and other creators.
How do I sign up?
If you already have a project started on WEvolt, just go to www.wevolt.com/
rumble.php?s=signup and select your project. Agree to the terms and conditions
and you're all set!
Does it have to be a brand new project or can it be something I've been working
on?
It can be whatever you want. An old project that is new to WEvolt, or something
new. Just make sure the pages you post fall in the date of the Rumble rounds,
otherwise they will not be counted.
What if I've already posted the pages on another community?
No worries, just repost to WEvolt. We are all about staying non-exclusive. It's
YOUR stuff you should be able to do what you want. Though if you want to win
in the Rumble, I'd push people here :)
How do i collect votes?
Convince your fans to click that LIKE button below your qualifying comic pages.
Maybe art bribes would work? Or something cool for your supporting fans? You
decide how to get people there, the more creative the better.
When they click the like button what exactly are they liking?
They are liking that page, and EACH page (up to 5 on M-F) is added up as your
total for the week.
I signed up last week and the weeks rumble has started, but I don't see a like
button under my project?
Make sure you have a page that has a publish date AFTER the 24th of Oct.
I didn't win this week, can I enter next week?
You will automatically be entered into the following week if you didn't win.
So if I win a rumble WEvolt owns my comic, right?
No, we own nothing. Your comic, your rights, your cash. Simple huh? Just tell
your friends about WEvolt!
What happens at the end of the rumble?
We hope that you've gained a bunch of new fans and keep using WEvolt! Also
get ready for the next contest which this Rumble is leading up to. More details to
come.
If I entered last week, do I keep my likes if I enter this week?
Likes are only based PER week to determine the weekly winners. However the
TOTAL for the round is taken from all your pages posted during the time of the
full Round. This could include pages you posted in EXCESS of the 5 pages /
week during the weekly contest.
Is there a limit to how many pages I can post in a day?
No, but we only count the pages with the top 5 # of likes for every week (M-F).
So spread out your content and post it next week. However with that said, ALL
your pages posted during the full period of the Round will be counted at the end
to decide the winner of the Round.
Is it better to upload all my pages all at once, or over the course of the week?
Over the course of a week, keep people coming back everyday.
Does my comic story need to start and finish up in the week, or can it be a part of
a bigger story arc?
It can be as big or as little as you want. However, the bigger the story and the
more frequent you update will give you a better chance at winning.
