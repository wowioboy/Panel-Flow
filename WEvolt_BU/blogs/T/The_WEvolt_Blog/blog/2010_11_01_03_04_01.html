WEVOLT'S RUMBLE SERIES WEEK ONE WINNER!

The first week of the WEvolt  Rumble wrapped up at midnight PST Friday night. The creator  response was amazing and the competition fierce. But after tallying all of the “Likes” we have an official winner.  We are proud to announce the official winner is:

 

 

 

 

HIGH SCHOOL HEROES!

From out of nowhere, High School Heroes stormed the first week of WEvolt RUMBLE SERIES!

In one week James not only brought HIGH SCHOOL HEROES to WEvolt, he brought his fans here. And they're not just any fans. These people were ready to click the hell out of any Like button that James could upload.

And click away they did.

James showed us that if you treat your fans right, not only will they come anywhere with you, but they will also support you when you do so.

FRIENDS OF LULU 2010 NOMINEE BEST FEMALE CHARACTER

This Friday, the Friends of Lulu, one of the most respected female cartoonists collectives honored the best of 2010. Our very own Carly Wagner had her macabre creation, CREEPY CARLY nominated for 2010 BEST FEMALE CHARACTER.

While RAMONA FLOWERS from Scott Pilgrim went on to take the crown, we couldn't have been prouder of Carly for stepping into the same ring as this Hollywood juggernaut.
