Creator Interview: Carly Mizzou

 

Today is creator interview Tuesday. Unfortunately last week’s Rumble winner, James Riot has already been interviewed. So whom to choose? How about CREEPY CARLY creator Carly Mizzou! Carly can’t participate in the Rumble as she’s the WEvolt community manager. But we thought she deserved some time in the spotlight.

1) How long have you been creating comics? 

Only since college. I was crushed by Stan Lee’s “How to Draw the Marvel Way” and the fact they took Wolverine’s adamatium away. So I fell out of comics and I rediscovered them through manga and found my love.

 

2) What inspired you to begin graphic story telling? 

I’ve always loved drawing. When I was a kid I’d make picture books about unicorns and princesses. I’m an only child so I was frequently left to “play in my own head”, I think my comics are just an extension of that…wait I do Creepy Carly!

 

3)  Quick! Plug your comic like your life depended on it. 

A sadistic little girl finds ultimate joy in murdering and maiming the local teenagers and random pets, and it’s the cutest funniest thing you’ve ever seen!

 

4)  Why did you enter the Rumble? How is it going so far? 

Ha ha conflict of interests there. I’m not only a Hair Club member, I’m also the community manager. (replace “Hair Club” with “WEvolt”)

 

5)  Could you share some of your strategy for The Rumble? 

Well if I could enter I’d most certainly update as often as possible, whether it be with content or blogs. I’d also make sure to have a bit of info about myself in my profile, THEN I’d facebook the hell outta all of that.  Also have some fun on twitter, and spend about $10 a week on project wonderful ads for my comic. It increases your audience 10 fold!

  

6)  Any unexpected results from your participation in The Rumble? 

Actually, YES! I’ve met a lot of really great new artists, and started reading a lot of comics I normally wouldn’t be attracted to. I’m not so much for super hero or space adventure stuff, but I have a couple that I now read!
