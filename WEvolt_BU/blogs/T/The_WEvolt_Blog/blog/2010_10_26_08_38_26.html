5 Easy Stips to Getting Started

Wanna get rolling on the WEvolt Weekly Rumble but you haven’t set up your project yet?  Have comic pages scanned, queued, and ready to enter the ring?  Run through the following 5 steps to get your comic LIVE on WEvolt as fast as possible! It's time to rumble...

 

 

1) CREATE

Once you have logged in, click the Create link in the site's main navigation at the top. This will take you to the main dashboard of the Project CMS. To start a new project, now click the Create tab in the upper right of the CMS navigation.

2) DETAILS

Enter your title, choose your Format (Image), and a sub category (Comic Series/Graphic Novel or Comic Strip)

3) TEMPLATE

Choose a template; if your comic contains anything considered 18+ content you MUST mark it age sensitive material; Optional fields include Genre and Update schedule. Click the “Next” button on the upper left

4) THUMBNAIL

Choose a thumbnail for your comic and upload; It should be at least 300x300 jpg, gif or png file type, click the upload button. DO NOT HIT THE BACK BUTTON. When the image finishes uploading you’ll be given options to crop as a square.

5) UPLOAD PAGES

To get started uploading your comic pages, from your project dashboard click edit pages from the lower left menu. From here you can do a single update (Add Page) or a (Batch Upload) for multiple pages

You do have a full set of tools available to customize your comic included with your free account, or even more if you decide to go pro.  Check here for more details on what a pro account gets you!
