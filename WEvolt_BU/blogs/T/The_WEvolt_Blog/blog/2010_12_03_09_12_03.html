CONTENT ACCESS CONTROL

On this high note we have ANOTHER FEATURE to roll out for PRO USERS. This one is amazing and extremely useful to Rumblers. It’s the addition of the Content Access Control. What does that mean? You get to designate which readers get what content.There are three settings Public (everyone), Fans(following), Super Fans(paid readers). You can set access for individual content pieces (pages, gallery images, blog posts, downloads) or entire content sections., like the entire blog.

How Does This Help Me?:

Now you can reward readers that become following fans, and give additional bonuses to the people that are PAID readers. Think about it, become a fan (follow the comic) get a custom avatar. Become a SuperFan (paid users) get bonus comics, desktop wallpapers, mobile wallpapers, avatars, custom art…whatever you can think of! If you're a PRO USER (click here to get more information), use your PITCH PAGE under your SETTINGS click EDIT INFO to tell your fans what content schedule you're committing to so they will know what they will get whensubscribe to you.

How to Use Content Access Control:

Click on PROJECTS up the top, then once you're in the Content Management System (CMS), click on the project you want to edit. Then click on “Manage Content” which is at the top of the list in the bottom left corner.

1)   If you want to change permissions on an entire content section (like a whole blog or gallery) click “edit template settings” To the Right under the “Section Title/URL” you’ll see the “Access Control” drop down. This is where you set who can view the entire section (and all the content in it)

2)  If you want to change permissions on single pieces of content (like single blog entry or a single image or download) click “list content” under the section the content exists.  Find the content you wish to change access on in the list and click edit. The content edit screen will appear. You will find the “Privacy Setting” drop down at the bottom of the edit options. This is where you set who can view this piece of content

There you are! If you have any difficulty with this don’t forget you can always post your questions in the forum, or directly email the community manager  carly(at)wevolt.com.
