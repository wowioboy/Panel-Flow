Tips for Experienced Creators

New to WEvolt but an old hand at webcomics? Great! Thanks for joining our community! This blog post is for you. We've got a couple of tips to help you get the most out of WEvolt.

 

WEvolt generates revenue for PROFESSIONAL creators by readers subscribing to them for $2 per month. We take a buck and remove all the ads, and you get the other buck. Here's three quick tips to getting your readers to subscribe to you. We call it the three C's:

 

1)   CONSISTENCY: We really recommend that you don't upload all your content at once. Establish a regular update schedule and stick to it. Reliability and consistency is the key to getting readers to subscribe to you. (Also, if you're in the Rumble we only count 5 pages per week, so save some pages for next week!)

 

2)   CONSOLIDATION: There's no need to send your audience to an archive on another hosting site. Why divide your hits and play your hand (see above)? If you’re a pro user, WEvolt provides you with a syndication widget allowing you to continue updating on those sites while consolidating your audience in one place. Focus your efforts where you can generate revenue, and audience will go there.

 

3)   COMMUNICATION: If you're posting old content, make it new by adding comments like a director's commentary. Just a few sentences with your pages helps people see that there is a person behind the comic they love.  The more you can let them know that there's someone behind the curtain, the more they will come back, chat and listen to any of your requests. eg. Rumble help!

 

That should get you to a good start on WEvolt. The key to becoming an online professional publishing powerhouse is Consistency, Consolidation and Communication.
