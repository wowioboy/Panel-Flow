I feel it must be said that you have free will. You should not feel in any way obligated to agree with me, especially if on a particular subject, my voice is the only one that you have heard.

I do wish for you to take me as someone with something to say, I’d very much enjoy it if you were to agree with me, and I’d very much appreciate your serious consideration of the things said, but, in the end, the decision lies with you as to what you shall make of what it is that I have written.
